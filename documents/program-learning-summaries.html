The following summaries reflect key insights and competencies developed through my undergraduate coursework in the Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration program at American Public University System. Each course contributed unique perspectives and skills that have enhanced my professional capabilities and prepared me for leadership in business environments.

ACCT 105

Accounting Principles I

Course Overview

This foundational accounting course introduced the fundamental concepts of financial accounting, including the accounting cycle, financial statement preparation, and the analysis of business transactions. The course emphasized the importance of accurate financial reporting and its role in business decision-making.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding of the complete accounting cycle from journal entries through financial statement preparation
  • Ability to analyze and record business transactions using double-entry bookkeeping
  • Proficiency in preparing and interpreting income statements, balance sheets, and statements of cash flows
  • Knowledge of internal controls and their importance in maintaining financial integrity

Professional Application

The accounting principles learned in this course directly support my project management work by enabling me to understand project budgets, analyze financial performance metrics, and communicate effectively with finance stakeholders. This foundation enhances my ability to make data-driven decisions that align with organizational financial objectives.

BUSN 410

Business Law

Course Overview

This course examined the legal frameworks governing business operations, including contract law, employment regulations, business organizations, and ethical considerations in business decision-making. The course emphasized the practical application of legal principles to real-world business situations.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding of contract formation, performance, and remedies for breach
  • Knowledge of employment law including discrimination, workplace safety, and labor relations
  • Familiarity with different business structures and their legal implications
  • Ability to identify legal risks and implement appropriate mitigation strategies

Professional Application

Business law knowledge is essential in my consulting and project management roles where I regularly work with contracts, vendor agreements, and compliance requirements. Understanding legal frameworks helps me identify potential risks, ensure proper documentation, and protect organizational interests while maintaining ethical business practices.

COMM 120

Business Communications

Course Overview

This course enhanced professional communication skills across multiple modalities, including written correspondence, oral presentations, and cross-cultural business communication. The course emphasized clarity, professionalism, and audience-appropriate messaging.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Proficiency in professional business writing including memos, reports, and proposals
  • Skills in developing and delivering effective presentations
  • Understanding of cross-cultural communication challenges and strategies
  • Ability to adapt communication style to different audiences and purposes

Professional Application

Effective communication is central to project management success. The skills developed in this course enhance my ability to clearly articulate project objectives, document requirements, facilitate stakeholder meetings, and present status updates to executive leadership. Clear communication reduces misunderstandings and drives project success.
